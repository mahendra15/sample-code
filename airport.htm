Nagpur is well connected by daily direct flights to and from Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune and Indore. Geographically Nagpur is centrally located which makes Nagpur Air Traffic Control (ATC) as the busiest in India. To uplift the Airport’s facilities, the second runway is under construction and is due to open by next year.
