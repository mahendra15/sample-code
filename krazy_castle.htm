Krazy Castle

Opp. Ambazari Lake, Ambazari Road, Nagpur, Maharashtra

10.00 AM

6.7 km. (from zero mile stone)

Krazy Castle Aqua Park is one of the most aspiring entertainment zones for tourist across Central India. It is the largest theme park in entire geography of Central India. Krazy Castle Aqua Park is a theme based Park designed for guests of all age groups. For Nagpurians the park has turned out be the hottest venue for celebration and parties.
