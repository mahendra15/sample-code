Getting to and from Nagpur by Road

Description :

Being the second capital of Maharashtra, Nagpur is well connected to other neighboring cities through a network of state and national highways. Some of the major cities of India are easily accessible like:

  • Hyderabad- 6.5 hours drive (493 Kms),
  • Pune- 11 hours drive (729 Kms),
  • Jabalpur- 4 hours drive (274 Kms),
  • Raipur- 4 hours drive (285 Kms),
  • Bhopal- 5.5 hours drive (353 kms).
