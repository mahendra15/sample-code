Nagpur Railway Station

Station Road, Nagpur, Maharashtra

1.2 km. (from zero mile stone)

The city is the Divisional Head Quarters of the Central Railway and South East Central Railway Zone of Indian Railways. It constitutes a very significant part of the transport system in the city of Nagpur in Maharashtra. The railway station has a central location in the entire country and hence is strategically very important not only in the state but in the entire subcontinent of India.
