Deogarh Fort

Chhindwara District, Madhya Pradesh

10.00 AM

120 km. (from zero mile stone)

Deogarh Fort in Chhindwara district, Madhya Pradesh, is located close to Nagpur. History records the site from the time of the rule of Bhakth Bulund King, whose kingdom spread over the Satpura ranges with "Devgarh" as capital. King Jatav is credited to have built Devgarh fort. The numerous wells, tanks and buildings suggest that the capital extended over a large area.
