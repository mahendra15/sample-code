Pench National Park

Seoni District, Madhya Pradesh

6.00 am to 11.00 am and 3.00 pm to 6.00 pm

159 km. (from zero mile stone)

The Pench National Park is the most popular tourist destination of Nagpur, situated between latitude 21°40' in the North and longitude 79°15' in the east covering the total area of 542.66 square kms. It is located just 95 kms away from the city of Nagpur. The Pench National Park was declared as Wildlife Sanctuary in 1977 and as national park in 1983. In 1992, the Pench National park became the 19th Tiger reserve of country.

Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve

Chandrapur, Maharashtra

6.00 am to 11.00 am and 3.00 pm to 6.00 pm

144 km. (from zero mile stone)

Tadoba is located in the midst of a reserved forest in Chandrapur district. Due to it's incredible natural beauty, Tadoba is also known as the 'Jewel of Vidarbha'. Covering a land area of 625 sq.km, which includes two forest rectangles of the Tadoba and Andhari Range. There are around 50 Tigers, Leopards, Sloth Bears, Gaur, Wild Dogs, Hyenas, Civet, Jungle cats, and many species of Indian deer including - Sambar, Cheetal, Nilgai, and Barking Deer.

Nagzira Wildlife Sanctuary

Bhandara, Maharashtra

7.00 am to 6.00 pm

119 km. (from zero mile stone)

Nagzira Wildlife Sanctuary is one of the best National Parks of Nagpur, located at Tirora Range of Bhandara Forest Division that comes under the Bhandara District of Maharashtra. This beautiful National park is just 2 hours from Nagpur and is believed to be one of most beautiful weekend getaways. Those who wish to explore nature and its various facets, should definitely visit this Majestic place.
