Shree Ganesh Mandir Tekdi

Station Road, Sitabuldi, Nagpur, Maharashtra

06.00 AM

1.3 km. (from zero mile stone)

Tekdi Ganpati, one of the most popular Ganesh temples in Nagpur draws numerous devotees every day. Located just adjacently opposite to the Nagpur Railway Station, festive occasions and religious ceremonies get thousands of people visiting the shrine that is believed to be a miraculous spot. The devotees vouch that their wishes are fulfilled here.
