Ramtek Ram Mandir

Ramtek, Maharashtra

06.00 AM

48.2 km. (from zero mile stone)

Ramtek town is the site of historic temples of Lord Ram and Lakshman believed to have been built in the 14th century. Ramtek was the place where Lord Ram rested when he was in exile. Located on average elevations of 345 metres these ancient temples are built of stone. There are 27 Brahmanical style temples on top of the hill.
