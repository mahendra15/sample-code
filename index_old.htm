Welcome to Nagpurlife.com

Nagpur is the largest city in central India and if you’re coming to Nagpur for business or pleasure, we can help you with concierge services such as car hire, meet & greet, airport transfers, hotel bookings, meeting rooms, city and businesses orientation and secretarial services.

We are based in Gayatri Nagar, one of the IT hubs of Nagpur only 10 minutes from the airport and 25 minutes from MIHAN. We have been working exclusively with overseas clients for the past 10 years through our sister company Com1 UK based in London since 1997. All our concierge staff speak English fluently and are only too aware that visiting Nagpur or any city for the first time can be made so much more enjoyable by speaking to people who live and breathe it every day.

We have an IT services division in Nagpur and four of our staff work exclusively with senior management in various companies from London, New Zealand and Japan so we are extremely confident in our ability to service worldwide Executives.

We have experience in commercial research, property development, IT software and support and we take pride in our ability to smile whatever challenges we are set.

Whatever you’re looking for, if it happens in Nagpur or the surrounding area then think of a question and phone us or send an email, we are here to help.
