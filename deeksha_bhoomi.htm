Deeksha Bhoomi

Ramdaspeth, Nagpur, Maharashtra

10.00 AM

3.3 km. (from zero mile stone)

Deekshabhoomi is a sacred monument of Buddhism and is also the place where Babasaheb Ambedkar converted to Buddhism along with his about 380,000 followers on October 14, 1956. It's the biggest "stupa" in Asia. This historical monument is currently nurtured and developed by Buddhist Society of India & Trust. This sculpture is world known for its beautiful design and architect.
